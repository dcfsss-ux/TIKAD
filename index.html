TANAW Filipino · Bisaya "To Look" · "To See"

Derived from the Filipino and Bisaya word meaning to look, to view, or to see from a distance — perfectly capturing the spirit of this smart campus navigation platform.

About the Project

The Name Behind the Map

TANAW is more than a campus map. It is a vision — a platform designed to help every student, faculty member, and visitor navigate Caraga State University with ease and confidence.

Developed as an internship project by students of CSU, TANAW brings the campus into the digital age with an interactive 3D map that puts every building and facility at your fingertips.

Our goal is simple: see your campus, know your campus.

What TANAW Offers

Everything You Need to Navigate CSU

Built with students in mind, TANAW makes it easy to find any building, office, or facility on campus.

Interactive 3D Map

Explore the campus in immersive 3D — rotate, zoom, and navigate every corner of CSU.

Smart Search

Quickly find any building, office, or department with real-time search and filtering.

Building Info

Tap any building to see detailed information — hours, facilities, and more.

Mobile Friendly

Access TANAW from any device. Optimized for phones, tablets, and desktops.
